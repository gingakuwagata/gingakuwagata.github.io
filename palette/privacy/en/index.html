Palette Privacy Policy

Original

Yuto Kuwabara (hereinafter "Provider") takes the utmost care in managing the personal information entrusted to Palette (hereinafter "App").

Personal Information

Personal information refers to information about an individual that can identify a specific person through details such as name, date of birth, phone number, email address, or other descriptions contained therein (including information that can be easily combined with other information to identify a specific individual).

Use of Personal Information

The Provider uses personal information collected through the App for the following purposes:

  • Authentication when using the App
  • Responding to inquiries
  • Addressing violations of the App's terms and regulations
  • Delivering announcements and notifications related to the App

Collection and Use of Attribute Information and Behavioral History

The Provider may use automatically generated data during App usage — such as accessed views, country or region, user agent, and OS version — in a form that does not identify specific individuals, for the purpose of improving the Service.

Disclosure of Personal Information to Third Parties

The Provider will not disclose or provide personal information (including statistical data processed so that individuals cannot be identified) to third parties without the prior consent of the individual. However, in the following cases, the Provider may disclose the minimum necessary information after identifying the recipient and the content of disclosure:

  • When required by law

Management and Security of Personal Information

The Provider maintains the accuracy of personal information and manages it securely. The Provider implements appropriate information security measures to prevent the loss, destruction, falsification, or leakage of personal information.

Third-Party Tools

The App uses AdMob (Google Inc.) as an advertising delivery tool, and AdMob may automatically collect certain user information. For details on the information collected, its purposes, and disclosure to third parties, please refer to the advertising provider's application privacy policy.

Amendments to This Privacy Policy

The Provider will periodically review the management of personal information, strive for continuous improvement, and may revise this Policy as necessary. Any revised Privacy Policy will be published on this site as needed.

Procedures for Disclosure of Personal Information

The App will respond without delay to requests from individuals regarding notification of the purpose of use, disclosure, correction, addition or deletion of content, suspension of use, erasure, or suspension of third-party provision of their personal information (hereinafter "Disclosure Requests").
